About Me

Hi, My name is Sravya Vujjini. I have completed my Bachelor's in Computer science in 2016 and then worked in Amazon Development Centre,India for 3 years as a Machine Learning Data Associate and also as Digital & Devices Technical Suuport Associate. Currently, I'm living in Indiana,USA and working towards my future. I'm interested in pursuing Masters in Human-Computer Interaction. I beleive this degree and the knowledge that I gain will open pathways to careers in virtually every area of society. I want to take up an exacting position, either in academia or the industry, where I can perform research on challenging and demanding problems.

Experience

Amazon Development Centre

Amazon Digital and Devices Technical Support Associate

Worked as a Technical support associate identifying and resolving issues in various devices such as Alexa, Fire TV, and Kindle devices. One of the major challenges in the role was to recreate the problems reported by the users to identify the root cause. Trained Alexa assistant to recognize human speech with the help of voice interactions. Supported the development teams in improving the user experience for Amazon devices & services.

Amazon Development Centre

Machine Learning Data Associate

I had an opportunity to be an early member of a team developing Just Walk Out technology in Amazon Go stores where shoppers can simply grab items and go without waiting in lines for checkout. As a Machine Learning Data Associate in the team, I was responsible for data collection and using that data to train Machine Learning algorithms. I’ve engaged in process improvement to increase the quality, efficiency, and accuracy of the backend technology by reporting the issues with tools and software to the development team, when necessary, by adhering to Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) using computer vision and algorithms.
